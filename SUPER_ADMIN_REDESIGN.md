## Design Tokens (for redesign)
- Border radius: 0.75rem (12px) for cards
- Shadow: soft, subtle (not harsh)
- Spacing: generous (1.5rem between elements)
- Typography: Fraunces for headings, Inter for body
- Colors: Amber (#e8b058) for primary actions, Stone for borders
