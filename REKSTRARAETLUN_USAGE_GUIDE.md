## Tips

1. **Set up income first** - easier to see if expenses are covered
2. **Use yearly frequency** for taxes, insurance
3. **Use monthly frequency** for utilities, contributions
4. **Use one-time** for planned maintenance projects
5. **Assign income to owners** to track who contributes what
6. **Expand monthly breakdown** to spot cash flow problems
