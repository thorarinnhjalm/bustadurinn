## üî• High Priority - Obvious Next Steps

### 1. **Automated Expense Splitting**
**The Problem**: Beyond the monthly house fund, co-owners need to split one-off expenses (repairs, upgrades, special purchases).

**The Solution**: 
- Allow any owner to submit an expense with receipt photo
- Automatically calculate each owner's share
- Send payment requests via app/email
- Track who has paid what
- Integration with Icelandic banks for direct settlement

**Why It's Obvious**: 
- Natural extension of existing financial tracking
- Eliminates awkward "who owes what" conversations
- Could integrate with existing Payday invoicing system

**Complexity**: Medium
**Value**: Very High
